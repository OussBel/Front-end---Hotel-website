Our Story

The DreamPlace hotel has been found by the two brothers Mkwayne in August 1950. The two young brothers started their dream by taking a loan from a bank.

They initially bought a house and they began to rent it, but this experience was not successful and the two brothers was almost able to pay back the loan seven years later.

After this huge failure, MKwayne did not give up and 3 years later, they found a sponsor, who finally offered them the necessary amount of money to make their dream comes true.

The project started and continued to grow not only in USA, but also in Canada and Europe, and now DreamPlace hotel is considered as one of the most successful 5 stars hotels in the world.
